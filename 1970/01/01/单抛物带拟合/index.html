2.\(Cu_{2}Se\)单抛物带模型计算12

2.1 计算公式

  • 塞贝克系数与简约费米能级的关系:

\[ S(\eta)=\frac{k_B}{e} \cdot \left[ \frac{(r+2) \cdot F_{r+1}(\eta)}{(r+1) \cdot F_{r}(\eta)}-\eta \right] \\ \\ F_i(\eta)=\int_0^\infty \frac{x^i}{1+e^{(x-\eta)}} \mathrm{d}x \tag{1} \]

  • 霍尔载流子浓度:

\[ p_H=4\pi\left(\frac{2m_{d}^*k_B T}{h^2}\right)^{3/2}\frac{F_{1/2}(\eta)}{r_{H}} \\ r_H = \frac{3}{4}\frac{F_{1/2}(\eta)F_{-1/2}(\eta)}{F_0^2(\eta)}\tag{2} \]

  • 霍尔迁移率(非简并条件):

\[ \mu_H=\frac{1}{2}\frac{F_{-1/2}(\eta)}{F_0(\eta)}\mu_{0} \]

  • \(\mu_{0}\)为漂移迁移率 由此可以得到电导率

  • 电导率 \[ \sigma =pe\mu=\frac{16\sqrt{2}\pi ek_{B}^{3/2}TF_0(\eta)}{3h^3}\mu_{0}{m_d^{*}}^{3/2}\tag{3} \]

  • \(\mu_w = \mu_{0}{m_d^{*}}^{3/2}\)为权重迁移率

  • 洛伦兹常数(参考其它文献):

\[ L=(\frac{k_B}{e})^2\left\{ \frac{(r+3F_{r+2}(\eta)}{(r+1)F_{r}(\eta)} -\left[\frac{(r+2)F_{r+1}(\eta)}{(r+1)F_{r+1}(\eta)} \right]^2 \right\} \tag{4} \]
