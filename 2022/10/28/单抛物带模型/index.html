2.Cu2Se单抛物带模型计算1 2 3

2.1 计算公式

  • 塞贝克系数与简约费米能级的关系:

\[ \begin{split} S(\eta)&=\frac{k_\mathrm{B}}{e}\left[ \frac{(r+2)F_{r+1}(\eta)}{(r+1)F_{r}(\eta)}-\eta \right] \\ F_i(\eta)&=\int_0^\infty \frac{x^i}{1+e^{(x-\eta)}} \mathrm{d}x \end{split} \tag{1} \]

  • 霍尔载流子浓度:

\[ \begin{split} p_\mathrm{H}& =4\pi\left(\frac{2m_{\mathrm{d}}^*k_\mathrm{B} T}{h^2}\right)^{3/2}\frac{F_{1/2}(\eta)}{r_{H}} \\ r_\mathrm{H}& = \frac{3}{4}\frac{F_{1/2}(\eta)F_{-1/2}(\eta)}{F_0^2(\eta)} \end{split}\tag{2} \]

  • 霍尔迁移率(非简并条件):

\[ \mu_\mathrm{H}=\frac{1}{2}\frac{F_{-1/2}(\eta)}{F_0(\eta)}\mu_{0} \]

  • \(\mu_{0}\)为漂移迁移率 由此可以得到电导率

  • 电导率 \[ \sigma =pe\mu=\frac{16\sqrt{2}\pi ek_\mathrm{B}^{3/2}TF_0(\eta)}{3h^3}\mu_{0}{m_\mathrm{d}^{*}}^{3/2} \tag{3} \]

  • \(\mu_\mathrm{w} = \mu_{0}{m_\mathrm{d}^{*}}^{3/2}\)为权重迁移率

  • 洛伦兹常数(参考其它文献):

\[ L=\left(\frac{k_\mathrm{B}}{e}\right)^2\left\{ \frac{(r+3F_{r+2}(\eta)}{(r+1)F_{r}(\eta)} -\left[\frac{(r+2)F_{r+1}(\eta)}{(r+1)F_{r+1}(\eta)}\right]^2 \right\} \tag{4} \]
